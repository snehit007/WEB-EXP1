Dining Room

Things to consider while designing your dining room

  1. Layout & Flow
    • Ensure smooth movement between kitchen and dining area.
    • Leave enough space for chairs to pull out comfortably.
    • Consider the number of people typically seated.
  2. Table Selection
    • Shape: Round tables are great for smaller rooms; rectangular tables suit larger, formal spaces
    • Material: Wood adds warmth, glass feels airy, marble brings luxury
